    Embedded Systems

    It is a system that embeds both software & hardware for specific function that is built into a machine. Embedded systems are used in all kinds of devices, from cars to microwaves. The term embedded means "built-in" and the word system means "a group of interacting, interrelated, or interdependent components forming an integrated whole."

    They can make our lives easier by providing great features and functionality that we need. The future of embedded systems is looking bright with the introduction of new technology like AI and IoT which will continue to enhance what they can do for us.
    The future of embedded systems looks very bright. They are becoming more popular in the home appliance industry as well as in commercial and industrial applications.

    >Home Automation

    Automation for life style, it is a term that encompasses a broad range of technologies and systems that are used to create convenience and comfort at home. It is a system where you can control all the devices in your home with just one remote, app or device. It has been around for quite some time but it was only until recently that it became more affordable for the average consumer to purchase. The most popular devices in home automation include smart thermostats, light bulbs, plugs, security cameras and smart locks.

    Home automation is the use of electronic devices to control and automate a home. Home automation can be used to control many aspects of your home, such as lights, temperature, and security, can be accomplished by using a hub or a controller. These devices connect all the different appliances in your house to each other and usually come with an app that you use to control them.

    Rapid Prototyping

    Rapid Prototyping is a process that helps to create a model or prototype of an idea. It allows designers to create models quickly and cheaply.
    Rapid Prototyping is a process that has been around for years, but it’s only in the last decade or so that it has become popular in the design industry. It was originally used to create prototypes of products like cars, furniture, and other physical objects. However, as technology has advanced, Rapid Prototyping has been used less for physical objects and more for digital products like websites, software applications, and even video games.
    The future evolution of Rapid Prototyping will be driven by improvements in virtual reality (VR) technology. VR headsets are becoming more affordable and accessible to consumers every day. This will allow designers to use this technology as a way to test out their ideas before they put them into production- which could lead to faster development cycles and higher quality products overall.
    The training stimulator is a device that helps to train the muscles and is used by athletes and people who want to improve their performance.The training stimulator is a device which sends electrical pulses through the muscles, causing them to contract. This can be used for rehabilitation, or for athletic performance enhancement.

    Training Stimulator

    Training stimulator is a virtual reality application that simulates an environment for training purposes. They are used to train people in high-risk situations, such as firefighters. These simulations have been proven to be more effective and cost-effective than other methods of training.

    Virtual Reality (VR) has been steadily growing in popularity over the last few years and it's not hard to see why. It has the potential to make some aspects of our lives better, more efficient, or even entirely new experiences altogether. One of these VR applications is the use of Virtual Reality Training Stimulators (VRTS).
    The idea behind VRTS is that they can be used to simulate real-life scenarios in which individuals would need to be trained for, but with no risk involved at all. This means that VRTS can be used for anything from firefighting simulation programs for firefighter's training, airplane piloting simulations.
